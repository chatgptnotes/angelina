# Competitor Research — Interior Design BOQ/Estimation Tools

## 1. CostCertified (costcertified.com)
- **Key Features:** Real-time pricing, interactive quotes clients can customize, digital signatures, payment collection
- **Strengths:** Client-facing interactive estimates, good UX, integrations with QuickBooks
- **Gaps:** No AI extraction, no 3D visualization, limited material library
- **Pricing:** $49-199/mo
- **Complaints:** Limited to contractors, not interior-design specific

## 2. Buildxact (buildxact.com)
- **Key Features:** Takeoff from plans, estimating templates, supplier price lists, scheduling
- **Strengths:** Strong takeoff tool, integrated supplier pricing, project management
- **Gaps:** Complex UI, no AI, no interior design focus, no mood boards
- **Pricing:** $149-299/mo
- **Complaints:** Steep learning curve, overkill for interior designers

## 3. STACK (stackct.com)
- **Key Features:** Cloud-based takeoff, assemblies, bid management, plan viewing
- **Strengths:** Powerful measurement tools, team collaboration, cloud-native
- **Gaps:** Construction-focused not interior, no material visualization, no AI
- **Pricing:** $2,999-4,999/yr
- **Complaints:** Expensive, complex for small firms

## 4. PlanSwift (planswift.com)
- **Key Features:** Digital takeoff, drag-drop assemblies, Excel integration, custom formulas
- **Strengths:** Fast takeoff, Excel-like familiarity, one-time purchase
- **Gaps:** Desktop-only (Windows), dated UI, no cloud/mobile, no AI
- **Pricing:** $1,749 one-time + $500/yr maintenance
- **Complaints:** No Mac support, no cloud sync, outdated interface

## 5. Bluebeam Revu
- **Key Features:** PDF markup, measurement tools, collaboration (Studio), form creation
- **Strengths:** Industry standard for PDF markup, powerful measurement, multi-user
- **Gaps:** Not estimation-focused, no material library, no BOQ generation, no AI
- **Pricing:** $240-400/yr
- **Complaints:** Not designed for BOQ, heavy software

## 6. Houzz Pro
- **Key Features:** Project management, estimates, invoicing, 3D room planner, lead gen, mood boards
- **Strengths:** All-in-one for interior designers, huge product catalog, client portal
- **Gaps:** Limited BOQ depth, no AI extraction, basic estimation, US-centric pricing
- **Pricing:** $65-399/mo
- **Complaints:** Expensive for features offered, limited customization

## 7. Cedreo
- **Key Features:** 3D home design, floor plans, realistic rendering, basic cost estimation
- **Strengths:** Easy 3D design, fast rendering, good for presentations
- **Gaps:** Very basic estimation, no detailed BOQ, no material pricing database
- **Pricing:** $89-249/mo
- **Complaints:** Limited estimation features, more design than estimation

## 8. Foyr Neo
- **Key Features:** 3D interior design, rendering, product catalog, basic BOQ export
- **Strengths:** Beautiful renders, large furniture library, integrated design-to-BOQ
- **Gaps:** BOQ is basic list only, no rate database, no margin management, no AI
- **Pricing:** $49-149/mo
- **Complaints:** BOQ feature is afterthought, limited customization

## 9. Planner 5D
- **Key Features:** 2D/3D room planning, VR, material catalog, basic material lists
- **Strengths:** Consumer-friendly, VR support, cross-platform
- **Gaps:** No professional BOQ, no pricing, no estimation features
- **Pricing:** Free-$25/mo (consumer), enterprise custom
- **Complaints:** Not professional-grade, no estimation

## 10. Infurnia
- **Key Features:** Interior design + manufacturing, modular kitchen design, BOQ generation, CNC output
- **Strengths:** Design-to-manufacturing pipeline, Indian market focus, detailed BOQ for modular furniture
- **Gaps:** Focused on modular furniture only, complex, no AI extraction from documents
- **Pricing:** Custom enterprise
- **Complaints:** Steep learning curve, limited to modular/manufactured items

## 11. Autodesk Takeoff
- **Key Features:** BIM-based quantity extraction, model comparison, 2D/3D takeoff
- **Strengths:** BIM integration, powerful for large projects, Autodesk ecosystem
- **Gaps:** Overkill for interior design, expensive, no material library for interiors
- **Pricing:** Part of Autodesk Construction Cloud ($$$)
- **Complaints:** Too complex, not interior-design focused

## 12. QuoteBuilder / EstiMate
- **Key Features:** Trade-specific templates, quick quoting, material + labor calculation
- **Strengths:** Fast quote generation, trade-specific
- **Gaps:** Limited to specific trades, no design integration, no AI
- **Pricing:** $29-99/mo
- **Complaints:** Very niche, limited scope
